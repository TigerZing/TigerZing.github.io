  In smart urban management, security assurance through monitoring is a necessary requirement to give people a safe and convenient living environment. When considering the current surveillance object, the monitoring through camera system is increasingly common because the popularity of cameras is installed in many places in public buildings such as buildings and schools. school, hospital, street, etc. However, the systems that support surveillance through cameras are now being used with many constraints in terms of using-craft methods depending on people as well. as less effective in economic terms. For example, these systems are only used as a video data storage system collected through cameras located in places where monitoring is needed, and the new monitoring is only manual level - observed by humans. directly through the operating center [1]. The effectiveness of this form will depend a lot on people, so it is easy to make mistakes by observers at the same time monitoring data from multiple cameras, along with being economically inefficient when lost. Know more about human resources management. In addition, when searching in previous collected video data, such as finding a suspicious object, this will have to be done in a manual manner that takes time and manpower. In addition, some localities have initially applied some smart monitoring support systems purchased from domestic and foreign companies, but economically, the price is quite expensive (According to Tuoi Tre Newspaper. - The initial implementation cost for a separate monitoring system in District 1, Ho Chi Minh City is about VND 270 billion [2]), on the other hand, with the acquisition of software, it will be very difficult to expand the feature. as well as other costs such as upgrades and maintenance.

 Accordingly, in this initiative, we propose to build a smart monitoring system through the camera system with taking people as the central object, the system with the following functions and advantages:

  • (1) Automating the observation process - assisting people in observing such as warning when detecting suspicious actions (theft, violence, crowds - unusual gatherings) ), identify abnormal objects through the face, .....
  • (2) Automate the search process - assist people in searching on previously collected data such as: finding people, Find children lost through functions such as face-based search, characteristics-based search (such as appearance characteristics - look for people with red shirts ....,...)
  • (3) Easy to expand functions, using the most advanced techniques: the system has the ability to easily expand functions, in addition the applied technologies are the most advanced techniques currently (Intelligence Artificial -AI, Deep learning).

 The proposed system supports the most observers in monitoring and searching will reduce the cost of management as well as operation, saving time. In addition, with the mastery of the technology aspects will be easy to upgrade, adding functions, the maintenance of the operation as well as investment costs will greatly reduce compared to buying software from the party. third or foreign. In addition, automatic computerization through CCTV will contribute to reducing labor, reducing costs incurred for monitoring and searching expenses in real life.

The proposed intelligent monitoring system is applied a lot of the most advanced technology today in the field of image and video processing. By having the group initially build test applications to test the techniques technically, it has assessed the feasibility of the advanced technologies used. Some of our tests can be mentioned as:

  • (1) Applying techniques of Deep learning (computer learning techniques for the best results today in many computer problems) in finding people through face recognition, or through recognition Characteristics (attributes related to the appearance of the object to be searched) (see demo and description of ears [4]).
  • >2) Applying techniques for face recognition, identifying actions, finding objects in a surveillance camera system of a building (see demo and description at [5]).
